About Our Company:

At Catherine Maids & Caregiver, we provide professional, compassionate, and reliable domestic help for homes across Durban. From housekeeping to elderly and childcare, our mission is to make your life easier and your home happier.

2002 - Our Humble Beginning:

We started with a small team of dedicated maids and caregivers committed to bringing trust, care, and professionalism to every home we serve. Over the years, we have grown into one of the most trusted care service providers in Durban.

Our Story

Catherine Maids & Caregiver was founded with the goal of providing high-quality home care and domestic services with love and integrity. Our team has served hundreds of families with dedication, ensuring peace of mind and comfort in every household.

2002 - Foundation and Vision

Our journey began with a single goal: to help families find trustworthy maids and caregivers. With a focus on honesty, training, and empathy, we have built lasting relationships with our clients.

2007 - Expanding Our Services

We extended our services to include full-time and part-time care for children, the elderly, and housekeeping for busy families. Every service is personalized to your needs.

2008 - Building Trust and Excellence

Our caregivers and maids undergo continuous training to maintain the highest standards of professionalism. Today, Catherine Maids & Caregiver stands for reliability, compassion, and excellence in home service.

Our Values:

At Catherine Maids & Caregiver, our philosophy is centered on trust, care, and respect. We believe in treating every client like family and every home as our own.

We are passionate about improving lives through professional domestic help and compassionate care. Whether you need a daily housekeeper or a full-time caregiver, our trained staff ensures quality service with a personal touch.

Integrity, reliability, and empathy define everything we do.
